Yes. Each of the 7 sub-courses is available individually at ₹6,999 (+ 18% GST = ₹8,259). These are ideal for beginners, business owners, or working professionals who want to upskill in a specific area quickly without committing to the full program.

Yes. Tiju's Academy has a dedicated career cell that actively connects graduates with hiring companies in Kerala, GCC countries, and beyond. Students also build a live project portfolio during the course, which significantly improves their employability.
