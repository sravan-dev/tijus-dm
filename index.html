This course teaches how to plan, execute, and optimise marketing campaigns across multiple platforms while using AI for content creation, ad optimisation, audience targeting, and data analysis. It also includes automation techniques like CRM workflows, lead nurturing, and marketing funnels to improve efficiency and results.

By the end of the course, learners can generate leads, drive sales, and scale businesses online using a combination of data-driven strategies, AI-powered tools, and automation workflows.
